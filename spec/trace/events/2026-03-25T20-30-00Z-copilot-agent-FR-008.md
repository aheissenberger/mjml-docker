Proposed FR-008 to extend `POST /v1/render` with an optional MJML `options` object.
The requirement constrains request options to `fonts` and `keepComments` and documents why
filesystem-backed, config-backed, function-valued, deprecated, undocumented, and
contract-breaking MJML options remain out of scope.
